# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

**AEGIS by [zokastech.fr](https://zokastech.fr)** — https://zokastech.fr

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of positive behavior include demonstrating empathy, respecting differing viewpoints, and accepting constructive criticism.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, trolling, and publishing others’ private information.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate these standards. Serious cases may result in a temporary or permanent ban.
